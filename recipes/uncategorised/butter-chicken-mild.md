## Method

1. Heat oil and butter in a pan. Soften onion 5 minutes.
2. Add spices; cook 1 minute until fragrant.
3. Add chicken; brown 5 minutes.
4. Add passata; simmer 15 minutes; stir in cream. Season mildly.
